AMS-PME Undergraduate Research Poster Session

    The Additive Structure of Algebraic Valuations of Polynomial Semirings

    Timothy Chen, Guanjie (Tony) Lu, and Alan Yao (Mentored by Dr. Felix Gotti)

    Paper

    Our poster centers around two key factorization properties — atomicity and the ascending chain condition on principal ideals (ACCP). It is part of our broader study of the factorization and divisibility landscape of the additive structure of simple semiring extensions.
