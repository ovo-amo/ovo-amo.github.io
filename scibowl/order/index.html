Struggle to order lists in Science Bowl? This is for you!


A highly customizable app to practice ordering (the dreaded By name or number, order the following).


Select your options (see the ) or start studying! When typing your answers, simply input the numbers, like 2413.


All subjects but energy currently have questions. There is also another subject called integers, where you just order positive integers. Excluding that subject, there are currently a lot of* questions loaded, a portion of which were initially generated by AI, but partially fact-checked. Contact timothyc. on Discord with feedback! Created by members of the UHS Science Bowl team.


*This counts the number of unique questions, i.e., with distinct sorting criteria and elements to be ordered. However, because answers are randomized, the true number of questions is on the order of 1017, and it's very unlikely you will ever see a repeated question.
